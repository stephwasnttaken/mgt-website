AuraDose

AuraDose helps college students take their medications on time by syncing reminders with their real schedules and daily routines.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower students to maintain their health consistently without stress. AuraDose intelligently aligns medication reminders with class schedules, meal times, and natural daily rhythm — so wellness fits seamlessly into college life.

Why AuraDose?

Unlike traditional reminder apps, AuraDose adapts to real college schedules. It imports your calendar, understands your meal and sleep patterns, and schedules medications at times that actually make sense. With gentle wearable reminders and simple setup, AuraDose supports your well-being — without interrupting your life.
