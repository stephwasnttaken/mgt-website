Why AuraDose?

Unlike traditional reminder apps, AuraDose adapts to real college schedules. It imports your calendar, understands your meal and sleep patterns, and schedules medications at times that actually make sense. With gentle wearable reminders and simple setup, AuraDose supports your well-being — without interrupting your life.
